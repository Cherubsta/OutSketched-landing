Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Out Sketched is designed to be a simple, local, and private experience. We do not collect personal data and do not transmit any user data off your device.

Information We Collect

Out Sketched does not collect or store personal information. The app does not require accounts, sign-ins, or user profiles.

Data Storage

All gameplay data (such as scores or settings) is stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Analytics

Out Sketched does not use analytics tools, tracking technologies, or third-party data collection services.

Advertising

Out Sketched does not display advertisements and does not use advertising frameworks.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data.

Children’s Privacy

Out Sketched is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

iOS and tvOS

This privacy policy applies to both the iOS and tvOS versions of Out Sketched.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
